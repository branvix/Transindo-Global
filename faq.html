Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We specialize in shipping cargo from Asia-Pacific countries to Indonesia, including freight forwarding, custom brokerage, and door-to-door delivery.

Which countries do you ship from?

We handle shipments from major Asia-Pacific countries, including China, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, South Korea, Japan, and more.

What shipping options are available?

We offer air freight for fast deliveries and sea freight for cost-effective bulk shipments.

How long does shipping take?

Transit times vary based on the origin country, shipping method, and customs processing. Contact us for estimated delivery times.

Do you handle customs brokerage in Indonesia?

Yes, we provide custom brokerage services and handle all customs procedures to ensure smooth clearance and timely delivery.

Can you arrange door-to-door delivery in Indonesia?

Yes, we offer door-to-door services, including pickup from the origin country and final delivery to any location in Indonesia.

What are the shipping costs?

Costs depend on cargo size, weight, origin, and shipping method. Contact us for a detailed quote.

What types of cargo do you handle?

We transport general goods, machinery, electronics, textiles, and more. Contact us for special cargo inquiries.

Is cargo insurance available?

Yes, we offer insurance options to protect your shipment from potential risks.

How do I book a shipment?

You can book by contacting us via phone, email, or our website. Our team will guide you through the process.
